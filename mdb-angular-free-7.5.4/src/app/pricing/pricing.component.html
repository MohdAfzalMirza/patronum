What is a user?
A user is defined as one personalised email address (you@yourcompany). Group email aliases such as sales@yourcompany and support@yourcompany are included in the pricing and don't count as additional users.
How do I buy and pay?
To get started simply install Patronum from the Google G Suite Marketplace and begin your 14-day trial. If you want to speak to someone contact our sales team. After the trial ends, you can pay online by credit card.
What if I increase users mid-contract?
You only need to pay for the users you have at the beginning of your subscription. When you renew, your renewal will be based on your current user count.
